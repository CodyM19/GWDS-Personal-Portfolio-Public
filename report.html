Coming into this module I had no previous experience with creating websites and I had limited knowledge of UX and UI design. Now at the end of this submission I feel more confident with the work I am able to produce.

My 3 Figma designs are very simple and use very basic colour patterns. They came from me randomly doodling in my sketchbook. I did have other designs that I would have wanted to use, but because this was my first time using Figma and creating something like this digitally, I chose to opt for the more basic designs in hope that I could create something I was pleased with by the end of this semester for my website.

The website started out looking like my Figma composition, however as the module progressed, I became more aware of how limiting it was. For this reason, I changed the design more which is why its appearance is less like my Figma wireframe.

I am a 19 year old, first year university student studying Digital Media at The University of the West of England. I am originally from North Wiltshire, but I am currently living in Bristol for my studies. The transition into university was tough but I am dealing better with the changes now that I have settled in.

One of the hardest things that I have found is missing my families. Across my two-households I have 7 siblings, 6 brothers and 1 sister, of which I am the eldest. This has definitely given me much needed perserverance and patience to cope with all the troubles they bring.

My hobbies include gaming, art, and travelling. I love to experience other cultures and use these experiences to widen my perspective.
